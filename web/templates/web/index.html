Fake News Links

Cataloging fake news for the public good.

What is this?

Fake News Links is a free public API for collecting, tracking, and monitoring fake news articles. Our goal is to crowdsource a comprehensive, annotated dataset of URLs that contain primarily false information, but is presented as news. This dataset can then be used by anyone interested in making sure that the news they read is real.

What can I do?

Contribute a URL to Fake News Links. In doing so, you are helping to fight the spread of false information on the internet. You can also explore URLs that have been contributed so far.

Do I need to sign up?

Now, you don't need to sign up. When you contribute, the only information we collect, aside from the URL, is the date/time and the location from your browser (which you can opt out of). Including location like this allows combined analysis with other datasets.
